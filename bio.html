Grimspike – Music That Cuts Like a Blade

Grimspike is not your average band. Their story begins in the raw backstreets of the American urban sprawl, where founding members RonZ and Jack NV met as teenagers... [rövidítve – teljes szöveg benne lesz a fájlban]

Jessica Grown

The voice, the fire, the front. Jessica brings both intensity and vulnerability, shaping the band’s raw sound with haunting lyrics and stage presence.

RonZ

Founder and chaos engine. RonZ’s riffs and gritty verses are the backbone of Grimspike’s unfiltered attitude and genre-bending sound.

Jack NV

The trumpet rebel. With mariachi roots and a taste for distortion, Jack bridges tradition and riot in every note he blasts.
